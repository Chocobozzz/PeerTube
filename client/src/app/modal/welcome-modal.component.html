Configure your instance

Now it's time to configure your instance! Choosing your instance name, setting up a description, specifying who you are and how long you plan to maintain your instance is very important for visitors to understand on what type of instance they are.

If you want to open registrations, please decide what are your moderation rules, fill your instance terms and specify the categories and languages you speak. This way, users that are looking for a PeerTube instance on which they can register will be able to choose the right one.
